        Frequently Asked Questions

        💸 Do you charge for just part picking?

        Yes. For any custom build request where I help you choose the parts, the charge ranges from ₹99–₹199 depending on system complexity.

        📦 Are the prices shown including shipping?

        No. All prices on this site are for consultation and build services only. Shipping is charged separately based on your location and courier.

        🛠️ Do you build the PC and ship it?

        Yes, for full builds. After helping you with parts and approval, I’ll assemble, test, and arrange shipping. Charges vary with system price and shipping distance.

        💻 Can you help me choose a laptop instead?

        Absolutely. Whether it’s for gaming, editing, or college, I’ll find you the best deal and machine under your budget.

        ⌛ How long does it take after payment?

        For part picking/laptop guide: under 24 hours. For full builds: 3–5 working days including stress testing and dispatch.
